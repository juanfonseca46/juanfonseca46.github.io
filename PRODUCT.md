# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, hiring managers, and lead designers at fintech companies, banks, and product studios. They scan for signal — do they trust this person's judgment, process, and craft? They arrive from LinkedIn or a referral, spend 90 seconds forming an opinion, and reach out if something makes them pause.

## Product Purpose

A portfolio that functions as a proof-of-work document, not a self-promotional brochure. Juan Fonseca is a UX/UI Designer specializing in fintech and banking (design systems, user research, conversion optimization). The portfolio demonstrates work with data and outcomes, not promises. Success: a recruiter or PM reads one case study and immediately wants to set up a call.

## Brand Personality

Direct · Precise · Credible

Voice: a senior designer who demonstrates, not promises. Speaks in outcomes and metrics. No floreos, no "passionate about design." Confident without being aggressive, clear without being cold.

## Anti-references

- Generic "creative agency" portfolios with Awwwards-chasing animations and zero substance
- Canva/template-generated portfolios: generic layouts, stock illustrations, bland typography
- Fluffy "soft" designer portfolios: pastel palettes, hand-drawn illustrations, "I love solving problems" copy
- Bloomberg/data-dense aesthetic: this is about the designer, not the product

## Design Principles

1. **Show, don't promise.** Every claim needs a metric or a deliverable.
2. **Systems thinking in the UI.** The portfolio demonstrates design system rigor: consistent tokens, documented scale, no ad-hoc values.
3. **One voice throughout.** Tone, copy register, and visual language stay consistent from hero to case study to footer.
4. **Earn each interaction.** Nothing animates without a reason. Every motion has a stated purpose.
5. **Trust through constraint.** Fewer elements done with precision beats many done sloppily.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum throughout. AAA target for body text. All contrast ratios verified. `prefers-reduced-motion` honored for all CSS animations and transitions.
